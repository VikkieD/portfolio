Tempr is an app that is made to be easy to use but also to look and feel modern. The families that are using this app are new and still want that stylized look to applications they use everyday. The colours that have been chosen pop and stand out from usual app colours consisting of greys and muted tones. Fonts that were chosen are simple san serif type faces, Roboto for the industry standard and Arial Rounded for variety and boldness. Icons are used throughout the app but are mostly seen in the weather tab. All of these elements combined help make Tempr an app that all young families will find fun and useful to use.
